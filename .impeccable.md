## Design Context

### Users

Maple Bookmarks is for power users who intentionally hide the browser bookmark bar to reduce distraction but still need very fast access to saved links. They use it in focused work sessions, jump across many bookmarks quickly, and value clarity, speed, and low-friction keyboard-driven navigation.

### Brand Personality

Quiet, refined, dependable. The interface should feel calm and premium rather than playful or loud. It should communicate confidence and craft without stealing attention from the browsing task itself.

### Aesthetic Direction

A warm editorial utility aesthetic: soft ivory and stone neutrals, restrained maple-toned accents, sharper hierarchy, and subtle depth. The UI should feel more modern and memorable than a generic extension popup, while staying compact, legible, and distraction-light. Prefer light mode as the primary expression, with a carefully tinted dark mode counterpart.

### Design Principles

1. Prioritize speed and scanability over decoration.
2. Make hierarchy feel intentional through typography, spacing, and contrast.
3. Use warm, distinctive neutrals instead of generic gray UI.
4. Introduce boldness through composition and detail, not loud effects.
5. Keep every interaction calm, responsive, and keyboard-friendly.
